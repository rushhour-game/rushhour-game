How the Betting System Works

Each round starts with a short preview of the selected location. Players then choose their preferred betting option before the countdown ends. Once the live feed begins, a counter tracks activity within a defined area in real time.

Popular betting options include:

  • Under bets – wagering that activity will remain below a certain number
  • Over bets – predicting that traffic will exceed a set threshold
  • Range bets – selecting a bracket where the final count will land
  • Exact bets – attempting to hit the precise final number

Since conditions can change instantly, successful betting relies on quick observation, timing, and understanding traffic patterns.
