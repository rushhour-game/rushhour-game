The Rise of Real-World Gaming: How Rush Hour Is Redefining Interactive Entertainment

The online gaming industry has always thrived on innovation, but every so often a concept appears that genuinely changes expectations. One of the newest examples is Rush Hour, a live-data experience created by 155.io that transforms real traffic footage into fast-paced prediction gameplay. Instead of simulated reels or digital cards, this title uses actual street camera feeds from cities around the world, turning everyday traffic patterns into interactive rounds.

This approach marks a significant shift in how digital games can work, blending real-world events with instant online participation.
